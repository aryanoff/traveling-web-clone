National Most Popular Places

Taj Mahal - Agra, UP

An iconic 17th-century ivory-white marble mausoleum located on the south bank of the Yamuna River.

Red Fort - Delhi, India

a historic 17th-century fortification located in Old Delhi, India, that served as the primary residence for Mughal emperors for nearly 200 years.

Taj Hotels

The flagship Taj Mahal Palace is Mumbai's most iconic luxury hotel, located directly next to the Gateway of India.

Nainital, Uttarakhand

Nainital is a premier hill station in Uttarakhand, India. Famous as the "Lake District," it centers around the emerald-green Naini Lake.

Goa, Western Coast

India's smallest state by area, renowned globally for its sun-kissed white-sand beaches, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese-colonial architecture, and rich biodiversity hotspots along the Western Ghats.
